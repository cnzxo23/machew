About Indian Cuisine

Indian cuisine is an enormous collection of regional and traditional cuisines native to the Indian subcontinent. Heavily influenced by diverse cultures, religions, climate, and agricultural practices, every region boasts distinctive flavors and techniques.

  • Spices: turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala
  • Staples: rice, wheat (roti), lentils (dal)
  • Meals range from everyday home cooking to elaborate festival feasts
